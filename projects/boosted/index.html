CASE STUDY

Leading Design for Boosted's Website

As the first designer at Boosted, I led the effort to update its digital studio, and led a design team that was supporting.

Overview

Boosted Boards came to me with a challenge: reimagine their digital presence to match the innovation of their electric skateboards. As the first designer on the project, I was tasked with creating a website that would showcase their products while telling their unique story.

The Challenge

  • Create a visually striking website that highlights Boosted’s industry-leading products
  • Develop a content strategy that appeals to both skateboard enthusiasts and commuters
  • Build a seamless shopping experience that educates customers while driving conversions
  • Establish a design system that could scale with the company’s growth

The Process

Research & Discovery

I began by conducting competitive analysis and user interviews to understand the electric skateboard market and Boosted’s unique position within it. I discovered that Boosted’s customers valued not just performance, but the lifestyle and freedom their products represented.

Information Architecture

Based on my research, I developed a site structure that prioritized product features, community stories, and technical specifications. The navigation was designed to guide both new visitors and returning customers through an intuitive journey.

Visual Design

The visual language I created was bold and energetic, using high-contrast imagery and a vibrant color palette that reflected Boosted’s brand identity. Large product photography and immersive video content were used to showcase the boards in action.

Prototyping & Testing

I created interactive prototypes to test key user flows, particularly the product comparison and checkout processes. This allowed me to refine the experience before development began.

The Results

The new website launched to overwhelmingly positive feedback from both the client and their customers. Key metrics included:

  • 45% increase in conversion rate
  • 37% decrease in bounce rate
  • 62% increase in average time on site
  • 28% increase in repeat visitors

Lessons Learned

This project reinforced the importance of balancing brand storytelling with e-commerce functionality. By creating emotional connections through design while maintaining clear paths to purchase, we were able to create a digital experience that truly represented Boosted’s innovative spirit.
