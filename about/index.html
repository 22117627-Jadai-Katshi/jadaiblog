ABOUT JADAI KONGOLO

I'm a designer and art director who loves to tell a good story. With over a decade of experience in digital design and branding, I've had the privilege of working with innovative companies like Rivian, Tesla, and Boosted. I focus on creating meaningful experiences that connect brands with their audiences through thoughtful design and storytelling. Currently, I serve as an Associate Creative Director at Rivian, where I lead a team of talented designers in creating industry-leading digital experiences. I hold a degree in Interactive Media Design from Art Center College of Design and continue to push the boundaries of what's possible in digital and brand design.
